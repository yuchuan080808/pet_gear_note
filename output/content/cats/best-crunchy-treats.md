## What to Look For in Crunchy Cat Treats: A Practical Buying Guide

Before diving into reviews, consider these key factors when choosing crunchy treats for your feline:

- **Texture & Palatability**: Most cats love the contrast of crunchy exterior + soft center (a hallmark of Temptations). If your cat prefers pure crunch, look for single-texture formulas like Greenies or Friskies Natural Yums.

- **Dental Benefits**: Not all crunchy treats clean teeth—but some, like Greenies, are veterinarian-recommended specifically for dental health due to texture and shape designed to reduce plaque.

- **Flavor Variety & Sensitivities**: Chicken and tuna are widely accepted, but catnip-based or seafood medleys may appeal to picky eaters—or cause digestive upset in sensitive cats. Always introduce new flavors gradually.

- **Portion Size & Packaging**: Large tubs (30 oz) offer better value for multi-cat homes or frequent trainers; smaller pouches (3–5 oz) suit single-cat households or travel.

- **Price per Ounce**: Compare cost efficiency—not just sticker price. A $15 tub may be cheaper per treat than a $4 pouch if volume differs significantly.

- **Ingredients & Transparency**: While full ingredient lists aren’t provided in the product data, brands like Blue Buffalo and Greenies emphasize natural or veterinarian-recommended formulations—worth noting if dietary simplicity matters to you.

- **Calorie Awareness**: Treats should make up ≤10% of daily calories. Smaller, lower-calorie options (e.g., Blue Buffalo Bursts in 5 oz) help manage intake without sacrificing reward value.

Now, let’s compare the top-performing crunchy cat treats—based *only* on verified Amazon metrics and consistent user feedback.
